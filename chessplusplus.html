    • Chess++!!!

    A simple chess game made by me. Do you want a pixel-graphic chess game? do you want to play a light chess game with your family and friends? Here's Chess++ for you-comrades!

    How to play

    Pawns (it looks like a bald) moves one tile forward basically, and can go two tiles if they never moved. If a piece diagonally facing pawn, and it is front-way, it can capture the piece. It is a brave solder that doesn't know retreating, and bravely fights against other pawns. The pawns are as strong as other pawns, so they can't finish their battles until other pieces interrupts.

    Bishop (it look like got beaten by an axe on its head) moves and captures diagonally and infinitely. He is a royal sniper that never miss target, and moves unexpectabley. How amazing he is!

    Knights (it is JUST A HORSE) they go two tiles and turn 90 degrees and go a tile, so they moves like an L. and they can jump over pieces. He is useful for protecting and attacking multiple pieces, so he is often used for early game.

    Rooks (it's a castle) they move orthogonally and infinitely. he is a royal guard of the KING, so they are at the corner of the board. and they have special move, 'castling', makes rook move 2 or 3 tile to the king and king hides behind the castle, rook.

    Queen (it has crown) moves like a bishop and a rook mixed, so she is the most powerful piece in the chessboard. she is the ruler of chessboard, controls massive tiles, and took benefits of bishops and rooks

    King (it looks like a jacket) can move to 8 tiles around him, and he is the most important piece in the chessboard. and he is a coward, so when he gets attacked, it's 'check'. when 'check', you have to move your king or protect the king from the threat. otherwise, your king dies and game overs.
